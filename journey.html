Prospera New Client Journey

At Prospera Investment Advisors, we believe great planning starts with truly understanding you — your values, your vision, and what you want to achieve. Our onboarding process is designed to be thoughtful, collaborative, and focused on what matters most to you.

Through a series of three purposeful meetings, we'll take the time to learn your story, clarify your goals, and craft a personalized strategy to support your financial future. From the very first conversation to ongoing support, we're here to guide you with clarity, care, and experience.

Below, you'll find an overview of what to expect during your journey with us — each step designed to help you feel informed, confident, and supported every step of the way.

1

Initial Discovery Meeting

Get to know each other and discuss your financial goals, values, and current situation. This is a conversation — not a sales pitch. We'll learn about your personal economy and what matters most to you.

2

Financial Planning Session

Build a personalized financial plan using the information gathered. We'll work through your complete financial picture, identify opportunities, and create a cohesive strategy tailored to your unique circumstances.

3

Strategy Review & Implementation

Put thoughtful strategies into action. Review recommendations, make informed decisions, and begin implementation. From here, we provide ongoing support and regular reviews to keep your plan on track.
